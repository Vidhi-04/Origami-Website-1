Easy Origami Panda Instructions

Step 1

: Start with a square piece of origami paper.Fold the left edge meet to the right edge to make a crease,then unfold.

Step 2

: Fold the right edge meet to the center line to make a crease,then unfold.

Step 3

: Fold the right edge meet to the crease made in the previous step,then unfold.

Step 4

: Turn the paper around,Rotate the paper about 45 degrees.

Step 5

: Fold in half twice make creases and fold back.

Step 6

: Fold the top corner meet to the center point.Do the same on the bottom side.

Step 7

: Fold the bottom edge meet to the center line.Do the same on the bottom side.

Step 8

: Open.

Step 9

: Fold the bottom part upward along the dotted line.

Step 10

: Fold the upper flap downward along the dotted line.

Step 11

: Repeat step 9 and step 10 on the other side.

Step 12

: Fold the right corner to the right along the dotted line, then unfold.

Step 13

: Frist fold the left part to right along the NO.1 dotted line.Then fold along the No.2 dotted line.

Step 14

: Fold the left corner to the right along the dotted line.

Step 15

: Fold in half by folding the top part backward along the center line.

Step 16

: Rotate the paper about 45 degrees.

Step 17

: Open the top flap along the dotted line,then flatten.

Step 18

: Fold the top corner backward along the dotted line.

Step 19

: Fold the bottom corner of the upper layer upward along the dotted line.

Step 20

: Make an Inside Reverse Fold on the left corner along the dotted line.

Step 21

: Draw eyes and finished .
